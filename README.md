## 🔬 Research

<div align="center">

**M.Tech Thesis — IIT Palakkad (2024–2026)**

*Supervisor: Dr. Pratik Ghosal, Department of Mathematics, IIT Palakkad*

</div>

<br/>

Investigating algorithmic fairness models at the intersection of **Mathematics, Graph Theory, and Computer Science**. Analyzing EFX (Envy-Free up to any good) allocations under structural graph constraints using combinatorial algorithms. Implementing and simulating allocation algorithms in Python for empirical validation — directly applicable to resource allocation and optimization in business contexts.
